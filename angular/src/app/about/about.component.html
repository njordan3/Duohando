A new way to learn ASL

Interactive quizzes, lessons, and practice to make the learning process smooth. Handango offers a new way to learn ASL, signing in front of the webcam with real time results makes practicing exciting and fun!

Easy way to keep track of your progress

Your dashboard will keep things organized for you to ease the learning process. Lessons will become unlocked as you progress and Ace those quizzes. Whether you can practice for 5 minutes or an hour, you can always logout and return to your dashboard where you left off as Handango saves your progress!

Multiple ways of registering for Handango

Handango offers various ways of registering. No email? Thats ok, you can still register if you have a google or facebook account. You can always switch to a different account type within your profile settings! Sign up now!
